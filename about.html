Our label...

was founded in 1897 in London. It all started when Ben Mitchell made a doll for his daughter Erin. This little hobby began the era of our little dolls, which have already gone down in history. All dolls are truly unique. Each has its own name and passport with history. Valentino Fashion House sews exclusive outfits especially for our dolls. Chopard adorns them with precious stones. Each doll is handmade with love for you!
